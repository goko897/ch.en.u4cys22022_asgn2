About Us

Panjabi Dabba is a delightful catering service that brings the rich and aromatic flavors of Punjab, India, straight to your doorstep. This catering service is the brainchild of a seasoned Dhabba owner hailing from the heartland of Punjab. With a deep-rooted passion for Punjabi cuisine, this culinary expert has created a culinary experience that encapsulates the essence of Punjab's robust and delicious food culture.
Panjabi Dabba takes pride in offering a delectable assortment of Punjabi dishes, each prepared with the utmost authenticity and care. From mouthwatering tandoori delights like butter chicken and seekh kebabs to an array of vegetarian specialties like chole bhature and paneer tikka, every item on their menu is a flavorful journey through Punjab's culinary landscape. What sets Panjabi Dabba apart is not just the exquisite food but also the commitment to hospitality and presentation. Whether you're planning a family gathering, a corporate event, or any special occasion, their catering service ensures that your guests are treated to a memorable feast of Punjabi delicacies. The owner's expertise and passion for Punjabi cuisine guarantee that every dish is prepared using the finest ingredients and traditional techniques. Panjabi Dabba is the perfect choice if you're looking to infuse your event with the vibrant and delicious taste of Punjab. With their dedication to authenticity and a diverse menu to cater to every palate, this catering service promises to delight your taste buds and leave a lasting impression on your guests.
